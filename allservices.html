Our Services

We offer a broad portfolio of services that may be required either for Marketing & Outreach, Student Recruitment, School Connect Programmes, solving specific problems or achieving new goals for established institutions.

MARKETING AND OUTREACH

With an extensive experience (7 and a half years) in the field of Education sector; serving eminent clienteles of Corporate Institutions & Indian Universities; we at Team Elevator’s have a plethora of understanding in terms of marketing & outreach activities for your institute which not only helps the brand to stand out among the crowd but also our efforts ensure that our partners stand apart.

We recognize your strength, share your vision and aspiration of the brand and communicate them to the world in a way that elevates your brand image and visibility, boosts admissions, attracts better faculty and benchmarks of quality that the institution aspires to become.

Apart from having associations with various test preparation institutes from diverse parts of the country; we conduct branding activities through

  • Direct Engagement plans with Students, Parents, School leaders
  • Customized Experiences that showcase USPs and Attributes
  • Social and Digital Media
  • Workshops & Seminars
  • Webinars with Professionals
  • Immersion Programmes for various Institutions
  • Individual Counselling Sessions for various courses

Our preparation of a well-researched branding strategy and execution of plans work in tandem to ensure marketing success for your institute.

STUDENT RECRUITMENT

We design and implement the pattern of student recruitment by mapping the institute’s expected benchmark of requirements; in terms of student’s quality and target of enrollments that you may have given us; as we believe that a well design selection process can go a long way in creating a better brand and word-of-mouth for the institution.

We at team Elevator’s co-create the strategy and execution plan with a clear definition of the class profile that needs to be achieved; by developing or refining the selection criteria, processes and the scholarship metric & also working out the realistic targets and quality benchmarks based on thorough analysis of the outreach and admissions efforts of the previous year; the strength and limitations of the offering and the team.

We execute these strategies by:

  • Anchoring the design and refinement of the selection criteria and detailed admissions process
  • Hand-holding the execution of the sequence of effort leading to submission of applications, organizing the interview experience, communication of decisions, securing acceptances and ensuring retention
  • Overseeing and supporting the scholarship award process
  • Providing training and guidance to team on nuances of engagement with students and other influencers in the decision making process for the student

The quality of communication with students in any form or channel plays a critical role in converting prospective applications to registered students. Since our brand philosophy also entails that sincerity and uncompromising commitment to the student’s best interest can go a long way in achieving the goals & helps the institute to embrace their true brand value.

SCHOOL CONNECT PROGRAMME

In this fast-paced & ever- evolving world, we at Team Elevator’s wants to create an ever-lasting impact on the careers of the high school students; by constantly facilitating them with an increased awareness around emerging academic pathways for students. This is where prominent career counseling platforms such as ours will help bridging the information gap of the high school students’ vis-à-vis what’s best for their future aspirations.

Massive adoption of digital technologies such as the Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cloud technology have redefined the careers of the future. Liberal Arts has also opened up new frontiers altogether; & high school students are unaware of the new, future-focused academic programs available to them. This is where we want to have collaborative dialogues (amongst high schools & universities) around best practices for career counseling.

With a support of our eminent corporate clienteles, we can organize various Workshops, Seminars & Webinars with Professionals. Our Student fair and Workshop series aims to connect universities and aspiring students making decisions about their higher education options.
Our School Connect programme & events will help:

  • Thousands of students on their career preparation journey as they are designed to adopt a holistic approach to student aspirations
  • Delve deeper into (what and where to study?) question & participate in a collaborative dialogue on important issues such as access, affordability and community engagement
  • Evaluate the long-term impact of your career and college counseling department including its role in increasing the reputation of your school
  • Focused networking with high school leaders and counselors from across the country
  • Develop valuable relationships & networking opportunities with counselors, teachers and university leaders with a significant number of leading regional high schools as well as country’s higher education institutions
  • Showcase your value-added solutions at a time when increasing numbers of regional high school students are searching for best-fit opportunities to pursue their education and career aspirations

Our School Connect initiatives will help us create a more cohesive environment for students and academic institutions in their future endeavours.

CAREER FAIRS

Our career fairs bring together higher education representatives for a collaborative dialogue on issues that drive sustainable and inclusive counselling practices focused on student success.

With a view that career and college counselling is as important as preparing for management entrance exams, our career fairs aim to empower aspirants with professional development opportunities to ensure that students find the best answers to very important questions like “which career fields to choose,” “where to go for their post-graduation studies,” “current scenario of job markets vis-à-vis placement of institutes” and “how to make it happen.”

Through our flagship Career Mentorship Programme, Workshops & Seminars, Webinars with Professionals, Immersion Programmes for various Institutions & Individual Counselling Sessions for various courses truly envisions that every management aspirant in the country will receive the best-in-class knowledge from our well-resourced career fairs as well as from our counseling office.
