RETAIL MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME

India will become one of the prominent markets for fashion retailers due to a large young adult customer base, ever-increasing disposable income and relaxed FDI norms. There is increasing participation from foreign and private players which has given it a boost.

Even though there is a huge market, still the sector is dogged with various challenges which include price war, supply chain, competition with local retailers, not able to decipher their customer to name a few. These challenges make it imperative for the retailer to have the right talent pool which does not just have the knowledge but also matures and performs with minimal training interventions.

Bloom's Taxonomy talks about how applying, analyzing, evaluating, and creating something from what you learn is more important than just remembering or understanding it.

We at Elevator’s Academy believe that Business education should come with “LIVE LEARNING”. To take this thought forward we are collaborating with certain institutions for a PGDM/MBA program in Retail Management where with classes (span across 6 Trimesters) there is a parallel learning in the retail companies throughout the 2-year program. The same will be called the On-Job Training which starts with the Summer Internship and goes on till the end of the 2nd year.

The USP’s of the program would be “Earn while you Learn” and guaranteed placements in the Retail companies where the students would be doing their Internship/OJT. The domains in which the students will be provided job opportunities would be in Retail Store Operations/ Supply chain/ HR/ Buying and Merchandising to name a few.

The vision of the program is to have a program based on retail which gives the Organization a candidate who is not just academically strong on concepts but has the Hands-on experience as to what has to be done in the STORE!!

CAMPUS PLACEMENT INITIATIVES

Whether the economic impact of the coronavirus caused you to be furloughed; or limited your income, you might wonder what you can do now to prepare for a post-coronavirus job market. Whether you are not satisfied with your campus placement job; or you are in need of a job which can give a longevity in your career, & yet you might have been continuing that same old short-term job, now’s the time to break the monotony of your career.

Global pandemic aside and regardless of a recession, it’s always a good idea to build resiliency into your career to safeguard yourself when faced with disruptions in the job market. It's never been more important to keep building your skill set. The only constant in our workplaces is change, and professionals who commit to updating their skills are the ones that will be most adaptable in a post-coronavirus job market.

We at Elevator's Academy have come up with Campus Placement Initiatives which will be looking after the skill development, launching of courses related to current trends & collaborating with certain academic institutions for campus placements as well. So we will be providing these benefits under this Initiative:

  • Resume Session
  • 3 rounds of GD- 1 round of PI
  • Identification of Area of Improvement
  • PI with Industry Professionals
  • JD based Preparation

There are always more skills to learn, no matter what your industry or position is. We will do an audit of your skills to determine educational priorities. We will not only evaluate your technical skills but also your soft skills such as communication and time management prowess and transferrable skills—those skills that are valuable in a variety of positions and industries.

For the placements the expected salaries will be between the range (5-8 Lakhs p.a) for all specializations. Our intention is to provide stable jobs along with good career roles & not to target those industries which will give unstable profiles (In current trends certain Ed-tech companies are doing this).

GROUP DISCUSSION- PERSONAL INTERVIEW (GD-PI) & WAT

Business Schools are always in a quest to find the best management aspirants from the lot; of whom many aspiring to be MBAs. Pursuing the world’s most sought-after course is not just reliant only on the management entrance exams, but an integral part of the selection process is also dependent upon an aspirant’s performance in Group Discussion- Personal Interview (GD-PI) & Written Ability Test (WAT).

With our continuous & relentless pursuit on guiding the aspirants for the very best in their respective careers, we at Elevator’s Academy are also coming up with one of its kind of GD-PI & WAT Programme; spanned across 50 hours of training by Industry Trainers & Mentors; wherein the key offerings & pedagogies:

  • One to One Mentoring, Evaluation & Feedback
  • Customized Analysis & Personalized Guidance at every stage of preparation
  • Essay Writing Concepts/ WAT (Written Ability Test) Concepts
  • General Knowledge Concepts & Sessions on Current Affairs
  • Case Study & Case Discussion Concepts
  • 3 HR sessions on Human Resource Concepts & Personality Enhancement Sessions
  • Sessions on Management Concepts
  • Sessions on Domain Knowledge & Technical Expertise spread across Finance, Marketing, Economics & Design Thinking concepts
  • Enhancement of Soft Skills via Communication Concepts & Public Speaking/ Extempore Concepts, GD Concepts, Group Interview Concepts
  • Augmentation of Behavioural Skillsets via Personal Interview Concepts, Form Filling (SOP, CV Editing, Individual Analysis & Feedback, etc.), Psychometric Test Analysis

It’s open to all Management, Engineering and Final year students of all streams as well.

This Programme will not only lead the aspirants in getting into a good business school but it will also transform them completely towards the realms of corporate world, campus placements, job interviews, managing their own businesses & throughout their lives.
