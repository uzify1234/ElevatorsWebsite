Marketing & Outreach

With an extensive experience (7 and a half years) in the field of Education sector; serving eminent clienteles of Corporate Institutions & Indian Universities; we at Team Elevator’s have a plethora of understanding in terms of marketing & outreach activities for your institute which not only helps the brand to stand out among the crowd but also our efforts ensure that our partners stand apart.

We recognize your strength, share your vision and aspiration of the brand and communicate them to the world in a way that elevates your brand image and visibility, boosts admissions, attracts better faculty and benchmarks of quality that the institution aspires to become.

Apart from having associations with various test preparation institutes from diverse parts of the country; we conduct branding activities through

  • Direct Engagement plans with Students, Parents, School leaders
  • Customized Experiences that showcase USPs and Attributes
  • Social and Digital Media
  • Workshops & Seminars
  • Webinars with Professionals
  • Immersion Programmes for various Institutions
  • Individual Counselling Sessions for various courses

Our preparation of a well-researched branding strategy and execution of plans work in tandem to ensure marketing success for your institute.
