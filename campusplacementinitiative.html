CAMPUS PLACEMENT INITIATIVES

Whether the economic impact of the coronavirus caused you to be furloughed; or limited your income, you might wonder what you can do now to prepare for a post-coronavirus job market. Whether you are not satisfied with your campus placement job; or you are in need of a job which can give a longevity in your career, & yet you might have been continuing that same old short-term job, now’s the time to break the monotony of your career.

Global pandemic aside and regardless of a recession, it’s always a good idea to build resiliency into your career to safeguard yourself when faced with disruptions in the job market. It's never been more important to keep building your skill set. The only constant in our workplaces is change, and professionals who commit to updating their skills are the ones that will be most adaptable in a post-coronavirus job market.

We at Elevator's Academy have come up with Campus Placement Initiatives which will be looking after the skill development, launching of courses related to current trends & collaborating with certain academic institutions for campus placements as well. So we will be providing these benefits under this Initiative:

  • Resume Session
  • 3 rounds of GD- 1 round of PI
  • Identification of Area of Improvement
  • PI with Industry Professionals
  • JD based Preparation

There are always more skills to learn, no matter what your industry or position is. We will do an audit of your skills to determine educational priorities. We will not only evaluate your technical skills but also your soft skills such as communication and time management prowess and transferrable skills—those skills that are valuable in a variety of positions and industries.

For the placements the expected salaries will be between the range (5-8 Lakhs p.a) for all specializations. Our intention is to provide stable jobs along with good career roles & not to target those industries which will give unstable profiles (In current trends certain Ed-tech companies are doing this).
