RETAIL MANAGEMENT PROGRAMME

India will become one of the prominent markets for fashion retailers due to a large young adult customer base, ever-increasing disposable income and relaxed FDI norms. There is increasing participation from foreign and private players which has given it a boost.

Even though there is a huge market, still the sector is dogged with various challenges which include price war, supply chain, competition with local retailers, not able to decipher their customer to name a few. These challenges make it imperative for the retailer to have the right talent pool which does not just have the knowledge but also matures and performs with minimal training interventions.

Bloom's Taxonomy talks about how applying, analyzing, evaluating, and creating something from what you learn is more important than just remembering or understanding it.

We at Elevator’s Academy believe that Business education should come with “LIVE LEARNING”. To take this thought forward we are collaborating with certain institutions for a PGDM/MBA program in Retail Management where with classes (span across 6 Trimesters) there is a parallel learning in the retail companies throughout the 2-year program. The same will be called the On-Job Training which starts with the Summer Internship and goes on till the end of the 2nd year.

The USP’s of the program would be “Earn while you Learn” and guaranteed placements in the Retail companies where the students would be doing their Internship/OJT. The domains in which the students will be provided job opportunities would be in Retail Store Operations/ Supply chain/ HR/ Buying and Merchandising to name a few.

The vision of the program is to have a program based on retail which gives the Organization a candidate who is not just academically strong on concepts but has the Hands-on experience as to what has to be done in the STORE!!
